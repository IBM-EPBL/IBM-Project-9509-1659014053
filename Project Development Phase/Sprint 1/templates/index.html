LIST OF FERTILIZERS

Citrus decline, apple scab, mango malformation, guava wilt, fire blights, banana bunchy top and wilt, brown rots of stone fruits, crown galls, downy and powdery mildews are the destructive fruit diseases causing huge losses to the fruit industry worldwide.

Fruit Diseases

Examine individual plants for unusual symptoms, such as leaf spots, wilts, stunting, fruit rots, misshapen leaves, cankers and stem blight. Roots should be examined for galls, root rot and necrosis (dead areas).
